About EcoSort

EcoSort is an AI-powered web application that classifies waste into categories like plastic, metal, cardboard, paper, trash, and glass using a deep learning model. With just a single image upload, it helps users identify what type of material an object is made of and whether it can be recycled.

The motivation behind EcoSort stems from the urgent need for better waste management and sustainable living. Many people struggle to identify which materials can be recycled, and this confusion often leads to contaminated recycling bins and environmental damage. EcoSort aims to bridge this gap by making waste classification simple, smart, and accessible.

Whether you're an individual, student, school, or organization, EcoSort can help foster responsible waste habits and contribute toward a cleaner planet. By encouraging users to understand what they throw away, we take a small but meaningful step toward solving the global waste crisis.

Technology Stack:

  • Model Type: Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)
  • Architecture: MobileNetV2 (Transfer Learning)
  • Framework: TensorFlow & Keras
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
  • Accuracy: ~94%

EcoSort is created by Manya Jindal, combining a passion for AI, sustainability, and real-world impact. It’s not just a project — it's a step toward cleaner habits, smarter AI applications, and a more sustainable future.
